My current project is about multiple model federated learning (local clients handle multiple FL models at the same time). In this scenario, how to select clients, and assign them with different training tasks is really important to improve the performance of the system.

Fair Concurrent Training of Multiple Models in Federated Learning
Marie Siew, Haoran Zhang, Yuezhou Liu, Yichen Ruan, Lili Su, Stratis Ioannidis, Edmund Yeh, Carlee Joe-Wong,
arXiv, 2024
Supplementary material

In the real-world scenario, clients could contribute to multiple federated learning models' training at the same time. For example, a company may update their keyboard prediction model, speech recognition model, and other more federated learning models on your phone. This paper explores a scenario where each client is restricted to training only one model per global round due to limited computational resources. We propose a client-task assignment strategy to preserve the fairness across multiple training tasks under such constraints.

Efficient 3D Transformer with cluster-based Domain-Adversarial Learning for 3D Medical Image Segmentation
Haoran Zhang, Hao Chen
ISBI, 2023

We proposed a pyramidally downsampled 3D Transformer to boost efficiency and maintain high segmentation accuracy. We also proposed a cluster-based domain-adversarial learning method to exploit available domains at a fine-grained level.
